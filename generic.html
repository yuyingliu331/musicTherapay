Goal

We want to make music and wellness more recognizable among people by providing music services to Fortune 500 companies and wealthy startups that want to improve their employees’ wellness.

Current Situation

• The highest health care cost in America right now is mental disorders. America spend an estimated $201 billion on mental disorders like anxiety and depression in 2013, according to the new analysis published in the journal Health Affairs. That makes it the costliest medical condition in the country.

• Nearly one in four people will experience a mental health condition at some point in his or her life. However, negative perceptions surrounding mental illness often prevent people from seeking treatment, which can have serious consequences. Untreated mental health conditions like depression are the leading cause of suicide.

Solutions

• We create an online platform to offer music and wellness services including music adaptive classes, music relaxation sessions and music therapy interventions to online customers. We also connect our music therapists and musicians to the most suitable customers in their local regions. The potential customers can be employees of tech companies, financial companies, health care companies, any big companies and startups who are willing to improve their workers’ wellness. Also, the customers can be anyone who wants to improve self-care or just for relaxation and self-realization. We hire all types of music professionals to our platform through self-registration and long-term employment contracts, with plans to add international collaborations. We will also provide online training for music enthusiasts in order to prepare them for becoming our potential registered musicians if necessary.
